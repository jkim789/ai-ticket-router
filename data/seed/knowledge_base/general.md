# NovaTech Solutions - General Information

## Company Information

### What is NovaTech Solutions?

NovaTech Solutions is a comprehensive SaaS platform for small business management. We provide invoicing, inventory management, CRM, and online storefront tools - everything you need to run and grow your business from one platform.

### When was NovaTech founded?

We were founded in 2018 by a team of entrepreneurs who experienced the pain of managing multiple disconnected tools. Our mission is to simplify small business operations through integrated, intuitive software.

### Where is NovaTech based?

Our headquarters are in Austin, Texas. We have remote teams across the United States and serve customers in over 50 countries worldwide.

### Is NovaTech a public company?

No, we're a privately held company backed by venture capital. This allows us to focus on long-term customer success rather than short-term quarterly pressures.

## Privacy and Security

### How do you protect my data?

We use industry-standard security measures:
- 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- GDPR and CCPA compliant

### Do you sell my data?

Absolutely not. We will never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is yours. Read our full Privacy Policy at https://novatech.com/privacy.

### Where is my data stored?

Data is stored in secure, redundant data centers in the United States (AWS US-East and US-West regions). Enterprise customers can request EU data residency for GDPR compliance.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently deletes all your data within 30 days. You can also export all data before deleting. Data deletion is irreversible - please be certain before proceeding.

## Policies

### What is your Service Level Agreement (SLA)?

- **Uptime**: 99.9% guaranteed (excluding scheduled maintenance)
- **Support Response**: 24 hours for standard, 4 hours for priority (Enterprise)
- **Downtime Credits**: If we fail SLA, you receive account credits

Full SLA details at https://novatech.com/sla.

### When is scheduled maintenance?

Scheduled maintenance occurs monthly on the second Sunday from 2 AM - 6 AM EST. We announce maintenance 7 days in advance via email and status.novatech.com. Emergency maintenance is rare but announced as early as possible.

### What happens during an outage?

We post real-time updates at status.novatech.com and tweet @novatechstatus. Subscribe to status updates at the status page. Most outages are resolved within 30 minutes. If downtime exceeds our SLA, affected customers receive automatic account credits.

### Can I request new features?

Absolutely! Submit feature requests at feedback.novatech.com or in-app via Help > Request Feature. We review all requests and prioritize based on customer votes and business value. Popular requests are added to our public roadmap.

## Compliance

### Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes! We're fully GDPR compliant. We offer data processing agreements (DPAs), EU data residency, and tools for data export and deletion. Contact privacy@novatech.com for DPA execution.

### Are you HIPAA compliant?

NovaTech is not currently HIPAA compliant. We don't recommend storing Protected Health Information (PHI) on our platform. Contact sales@novatech.com if you need HIPAA compliance - we're evaluating it for future releases.

### Do you have SOC 2 certification?

Yes! We're SOC 2 Type II certified. Audit reports are available to Enterprise customers under NDA. Contact security@novatech.com to request our SOC 2 report.

## Getting Started

### How do I sign up?

Visit https://novatech.com and click "Start Free Trial." Enter your email, create a password, and you're in! No credit card required for the 14-day trial. You'll be guided through initial setup.

### Is there a setup fee?

No! There are no setup fees, cancellation fees, or hidden costs. You only pay your monthly or annual subscription. Cancel anytime without penalty.

### How long does setup take?

Most customers are up and running within 30 minutes. Basic setup (company info, add a few contacts, create an invoice) takes 10-15 minutes. Full setup with integrations and data import may take 1-2 hours. We provide setup guides for every step.

### Can I import data from other platforms?

Yes! We support import from:
- QuickBooks (Online and Desktop)
- FreshBooks
- Xero
- Excel/CSV files
- Many other platforms via Zapier

Import is in Settings > Import/Export > Import Data.
