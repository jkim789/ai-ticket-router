# NovaTech Solutions - Returns & Exchanges

## Return Policy

### What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in original condition with all packaging, tags, and accessories. Software licenses and digital products cannot be returned once activated.

### How do I start a return?

Log into your account and go to Orders > Request Return. Select the items you want to return, choose a reason, and click "Submit Request." You'll receive a prepaid return label via email within 24 hours. No return label fee for US customers!

### Do I have to pay for return shipping?

Return shipping is free in the US for defective products or our errors. For other returns (wrong size, changed mind, etc.), a $6.99 return shipping fee is deducted from your refund. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

### How long do refunds take?

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 2-3 business days. The refund appears in your account within 5-10 business days depending on your bank. You'll receive an email when your refund is processed.

## Exchanges

### Can I exchange an item?

Yes! Request an exchange instead of a return when submitting your return request. We'll ship the replacement immediately upon receiving your exchange request. You'll get an exchange confirmation email with new tracking.

### Is there a fee for exchanges?

Exchanges are free in the US if you're exchanging for the same product in a different size/color. If exchanging for a different product, you'll pay/receive the price difference. A $6.99 shipping fee applies for non-defect exchanges.

### How long do exchanges take?

We ship your exchange within 24 hours of receiving your exchange request. Your new item should arrive in 5-7 business days. You can return the original item whenever it's convenient using the provided prepaid label.

### What if the item I want to exchange for is out of stock?

We'll notify you via email with options: 1) Wait for a restock (usually 2-3 weeks), 2) Exchange for a similar item, or 3) Receive a full refund. You choose! We'll hold your return credit until you decide.

## Damaged or Defective Items

### What if my item arrived damaged?

We apologize! Contact support@novatech.com within 48 hours with photos of the damage. Include your order number. We'll send a replacement immediately via expedited shipping at no cost. You don't need to return the damaged item unless requested.

### My product is defective, what should I do?

Contact support@novatech.com with your order number and a description of the defect. We may troubleshoot with you first. If defective, we'll send a free replacement via expedited shipping. Return the defective item using the prepaid label we provide.

### Is there a warranty?

All NovaTech hardware products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects. Software subscriptions include unlimited support. Extended warranties (2-3 years) are available at checkout. Warranty claims are processed through support@novatech.com.

### What if my item breaks after 30 days?

If within the 1-year warranty period, contact support@novatech.com with your order number and issue description. We'll troubleshoot and provide a replacement if it's a manufacturer defect. Damage from misuse or accidents isn't covered by warranty.

## Return Requirements

### What condition do items need to be in?

Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition with all tags attached. Include all original packaging, accessories, manuals, and components. We inspect all returns - items not meeting these requirements will be returned to you.

### Can I return opened software?

Digital products and software licenses cannot be returned once activated or downloaded. If you have technical issues preventing use, contact support@novatech.com - we'll troubleshoot or provide alternatives.

### What if I lost the original packaging?

We prefer original packaging, but it's not required if you lost it. Pack the item securely in a box to prevent damage during return shipping. If the item is damaged in transit due to poor packaging, refunds may be reduced.

### Can I return sale items?

Yes! Sale and clearance items follow the same 30-day return policy. However, they're final sale during special promotional periods (clearly marked at checkout). Check your order confirmation for "Final Sale" indicators.

## Special Cases

### I received the wrong item

We sincerely apologize! Contact support@novatech.com immediately with your order number and photos of what you received. We'll send the correct item via expedited shipping at no cost and provide a prepaid label for the incorrect item.

### My order is missing items

Contact support@novatech.com within 48 hours with your order number and list of missing items. We'll verify your order and ship missing items immediately via expedited shipping. We may ask for photos of the package contents.

### Can I return a gift?

Yes! Gift recipients can return items for store credit (not original payment method). Go to Returns > Gift Return and enter the order number from the packing slip. The original purchaser won't be notified of the return.

### I missed the 30-day return window

Contact support@novatech.com and explain your situation. While our policy is 30 days, we evaluate late returns case-by-case for extenuating circumstances. We may offer store credit or exchange options.

## Return Process

### How do I pack my return?

Use the original packaging if possible. If not, use a sturdy box and pack items securely with bubble wrap or paper. Include the return slip printed from your return request confirmation. Seal well and attach the prepaid return label.

### Where do I drop off my return?

Drop off at any USPS location (Post Office or blue collection box). You can also schedule a free pickup at USPS.com. Hand the package to a postal worker and get a receipt. Track your return using the number on your label.

### How do I track my return?

Use the tracking number on your prepaid return label. Track at USPS.com or in your account under Returns > Track Return. We'll email you when we receive your return and again when your refund is processed.

### What if my return is lost in transit?

Return labels include insurance. If tracking shows your return is lost, contact support@novatech.com with your return tracking number. We'll file a carrier claim and process your refund immediately while investigating with the carrier.

## Refund Details

### How will I receive my refund?

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds appear in 5-10 business days. PayPal refunds are instant. Bank transfer refunds take 3-5 business days. Store credit (if chosen) is instant.

### Can I get store credit instead?

Yes! When submitting your return request, choose "Store Credit" as your refund method. Store credit is issued instantly when we receive your return and never expires. Plus, you get 10% bonus credit!

### Will I be refunded for shipping costs?

Original shipping costs are refunded only if the return is due to our error or a defective product. For other returns, shipping costs are non-refundable. Free shipping orders are refunded minus the actual shipping cost ($8.99).
