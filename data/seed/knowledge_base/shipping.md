# NovaTech Solutions - Shipping Information

## Shipping Times

### How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the US takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping (2-3 business days) and overnight shipping are available at checkout. International shipping typically takes 10-15 business days depending on customs processing.

### When will my order ship?

Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same business day. Orders after 2 PM ship the next business day. Weekend and holiday orders ship the following business day. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking once your order ships.

### Do you ship on weekends?

Our warehouse operates Monday-Friday. Weekend orders are processed on Monday. Delivery carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx) operate on weekends for certain service levels, but most deliveries occur Monday-Friday.

### Can I get faster shipping?

Yes! At checkout, select "Expedited Shipping" (2-3 business days, +$15) or "Overnight Shipping" (next business day, +$35). Shipping upgrades must be selected before placing your order. Contact support@novatech.com within 1 hour of ordering to upgrade.

## Tracking

### How do I track my order?

Check your shipping confirmation email for the tracking number and carrier. Click the tracking link to see real-time updates. You can also track orders in your account dashboard under Orders > Track Shipment.

### My tracking hasn't updated in days

Tracking sometimes shows delays during carrier processing or customs clearance. If domestic tracking hasn't updated in 3 business days, or international in 7 days, contact support@novatech.com with your order number.

### What if tracking shows "Delivered" but I didn't receive it?

First, check with household members and neighbors. Look for packages left in safe locations (porch, mailbox, garage). Contact the carrier directly - they can provide GPS delivery confirmation. If still missing after 48 hours, contact support@novatech.com to file a claim.

### Can I change the delivery address after shipping?

Once shipped, address changes must be made through the carrier. Use your tracking number to contact USPS, UPS, or FedEx and request an address intercept. Intercept fees ($15-20) apply and aren't guaranteed. Contact them immediately for best results.

## International Shipping

### Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 100 countries. Shipping costs and times vary by destination. International shipping options and costs are shown at checkout when you enter your country.

### What about customs and duties?

International customers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and brokerage fees charged by their country. These fees are determined by local customs authorities and paid upon delivery or clearance. We provide all necessary customs documentation.

### How long does international shipping take?

International shipping typically takes 10-15 business days, but can take up to 30 days depending on customs processing. We ship via USPS International, DHL, or FedEx International. Tracking is provided for all international shipments.

### My international package is stuck in customs

Customs delays are common and outside our control. Most packages clear customs within 7-10 business days. Contact your local customs office with your tracking number for status updates. Ensure all duties/taxes are paid promptly if notified.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

### What if my package is lost?

If tracking shows no movement for 7 business days (domestic) or 15 days (international), contact support@novatech.com with your order number. We'll file a carrier claim and send a replacement or issue a refund within 3-5 business days.

### My package arrived damaged

We're sorry! Take photos of the damaged packaging and product. Contact support@novatech.com within 48 hours with photos and your order number. We'll send a replacement immediately. You don't need to return damaged items unless requested.

### The carrier says my package is lost

Contact support@novatech.com immediately with your order number and carrier claim number. We'll expedite a replacement shipment at no cost. Insurance claims with carriers can take 2-4 weeks, but we'll resolve your issue within 3 business days.

## Shipping Costs

### How much does shipping cost?

- **US Standard**: Free on orders over $100, otherwise $8.99
- **US Expedited**: $15 (2-3 business days)
- **US Overnight**: $35 (next business day)
- **International**: Varies by destination, calculated at checkout

### Can I get free shipping?

Yes! All US orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping. Sign up for our newsletter to receive promotional codes for free shipping on smaller orders. Enterprise customers receive free expedited shipping on all orders.

### Why is international shipping so expensive?

International rates include carrier costs, customs documentation, insurance, and international tracking. Shipping carriers set these rates based on weight, dimensions, and destination. We negotiate the best possible rates but have limited control over international shipping costs.

## PO Boxes and Alternative Addresses

### Can you ship to a PO Box?

Yes! USPS shipments can be sent to PO Boxes. However, expedited and overnight shipping via UPS/FedEx cannot be delivered to PO Boxes. If you select fast shipping with a PO Box address, we'll contact you for an alternative address.

### Can I ship to a different address?

Absolutely! At checkout, check "Ship to a different address" and enter the recipient's address. This is perfect for gifts or shipping to your office. Billing and shipping addresses don't need to match.

### Can packages be held for pickup?

Yes! Most carriers offer "Hold for Pickup" services. After receiving your tracking number, contact the carrier directly to redirect your package to a nearby facility or retail location. This is useful if you won't be home for delivery.

## Order Issues

### What if I ordered the wrong product?

Contact support@novatech.com immediately with your order number. If your order hasn't shipped, we can modify it. If already shipped, you can return the item within 30 days for exchange or refund (see our Returns policy).

### Can I cancel my order?

Yes, if it hasn't shipped yet. Contact support@novatech.com immediately with your order number. Orders are usually processed within a few hours, so act fast! If already shipped, you'll need to return the item once received.

### I never received a shipping confirmation

Check your spam/promotions folder. If you can't find it, log into your account and check Orders > Order History for tracking info. If tracking isn't available and it's been more than 2 business days, contact support@novatech.com.
