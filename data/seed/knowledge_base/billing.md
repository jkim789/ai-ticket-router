# NovaTech Solutions - Billing & Payments

## Payment Methods

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers (ACH). For enterprise customers, we also offer invoice billing with net-30 terms.

### Can I update my payment method?

Yes! Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. Click "Add Payment Method" or click the three dots next to an existing method to set it as default or remove it. Changes take effect immediately for your next billing cycle.

### When will my card be charged?

Your card is charged automatically on your billing date each month. You'll receive an email receipt immediately after the charge. You can view your billing date in Settings > Billing > Subscription.

## Subscription Management

### How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Select your desired plan and click "Upgrade Now." You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle, and your new features will be available immediately.

### How do I downgrade my plan?

Visit Settings > Billing > Change Plan and select a lower tier. The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You'll retain access to your current plan features until then.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. You'll have access to paid features until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. You can reactivate your subscription anytime during this period to restore full access. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted.

## Refunds and Billing Issues

### What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new customers. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days of your initial purchase, contact support@novatech.com for a full refund. After 14 days, subscriptions are non-refundable.

### I was charged twice, what should I do?

We sincerely apologize! Duplicate charges are usually processing errors. Email billing@novatech.com with your account email and transaction details. We'll investigate immediately and issue a refund within 3-5 business days.

### How do I dispute a charge?

Contact billing@novatech.com with your account email, charge date, and amount. Include a brief explanation. Our billing team responds within 24 hours on business days. We'll investigate and resolve the issue or provide a detailed explanation.

### Can I get a refund for unused time?

We don't offer prorated refunds for mid-month cancellations. Your subscription remains active until the end of your paid period. However, if you're experiencing technical issues preventing use, contact support@novatech.com to discuss options.

## Invoices and Receipts

### How do I download my invoices?

Go to Settings > Billing > Invoices. All past invoices are listed with download buttons. Click "Download PDF" next to any invoice. Invoices are also automatically emailed after each charge.

### Can I get invoices sent to a different email?

Yes! In Settings > Billing > Invoice Settings, add additional email addresses for invoice delivery. You can add up to 5 email addresses (great for accounting teams).

### Do you provide tax exemption?

If you're tax-exempt, email a copy of your tax exemption certificate to billing@novatech.com. Include your account email. We'll apply the exemption to future invoices. Past charges cannot be adjusted.

## Pricing and Plans

### How much does NovaTech cost?

We offer three tiers: Starter ($29/month), Professional ($79/month), and Enterprise ($199/month). All plans include core features. Higher tiers add advanced analytics, API access, priority support, and higher usage limits.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! New customers get a 14-day free trial of our Professional plan. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime during the trial without being charged.

### Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Pay annually and save 20%. Annual plans are $290 (Starter), $790 (Professional), and $1,990 (Enterprise). Switch to annual billing in Settings > Billing > Billing Frequency.

### What if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll send you an email notification at 80% and 100% usage. If you exceed limits, you have three options: 1) Upgrade to a higher plan, 2) Purchase add-on capacity, or 3) Wait until your usage resets next billing cycle. We don't automatically charge overages.

### Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes! Registered 501(c)(3) nonprofits receive 50% off all plans. Email nonprofit@novatech.com with your EIN and tax-exempt documentation. Approval typically takes 2-3 business days.
